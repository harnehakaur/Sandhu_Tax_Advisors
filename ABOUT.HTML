About Sandhu Tax Advisors

A trusted tax and compliance advisory practice led by Harmeet Kaur Sandhu, focused on helping businesses and individuals stay compliant, confident, and growth-ready.

Our Story

Sandhu Tax Advisors started with a simple mission: remove the confusion around tax and legal compliance for local entrepreneurs and families. Over the years, we have supported shop owners, professionals, startups, and established businesses who needed timely and dependable advisory support.

What began as one-to-one tax filing guidance has grown into a full-service practice covering GST, income tax, registrations, and operational compliance. We believe compliance should not feel stressful—it should feel structured, transparent, and proactive.

Today, we continue to work with the same values we started with: clear communication, practical advice, and genuine accountability for every client we serve.
